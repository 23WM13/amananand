I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in the BAM Lab at the School of Computing, Queen's University, working under the supervision of Dr. Farhana Zulkernine. My research focuses on computer vision, with an emphasis on self-supervised learning and multimodal vision-language models for human-machine interaction.

Before beginning my Ph.D., I earned an M.S. in Computer Science from Indiana State University, where I worked on converting handwritten documents to LaTeX using attention-based models. I hold a B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from Amity University Kolkata.

Apart from research, I love to cook, and watch sci-fi movies.
